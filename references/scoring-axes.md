# Scoring Axes

Choose only the axes needed for the task. Scores are a thinking aid, not a substitute for judgment.

## General

- Impact.
- Feasibility.
- Cost.
- Speed.
- Risk.
- Reversibility.
- Learning value.
- Strategic fit.

## Ideation

- Novelty.
- Usefulness.
- Delight.
- Memorability.
- Personal fit.
- Audience fit.

## Technical Or Strategic Decisions

- Maintainability.
- Complexity.
- Operational burden.
- Security or safety risk.
- Migration cost.
- Time to validate.
- Failure blast radius.

## Personal Decisions

- Energy fit.
- Identity fit.
- Financial cost.
- Social cost.
- Regret risk.
- Optionality.
